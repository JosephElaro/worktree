## Core Maintainers

The W0rkTree core team is responsible for the design, development, and maintenance of the W0rkTree version control system.

### Mission

Our mission is to make version control intuitive, fast, and collaborative. We believe that version control should adapt to how teams work, not the other way around.

### Contributing

We welcome contributions from the community! Check out our [contribution guidelines](https://github.com/worktree/worktree/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) to get started.
